# References and Procedures

ME distinguishes Cognitions from other local material.

## Cognition

Something you explicitly chose to keep in ME.

## Reference

Local material Codex may consult for a task.

Examples include notes, policy documents, source excerpts, or corpus
files.

References are not Cognitions and are not bulk-promoted into ME.

## Procedure

Optional instructions for a repeated workflow.

New workspaces include example Procedures:

```text
procedures/inspect-me.md
procedures/speak-from-me.md
```

Procedures are not Apps and are not canonical ME objects. They are
ordinary local files that Codex may read when relevant.

## Boundary

If a Reference or Procedure contains a sentence worth keeping, capture
that sentence as a Thought and approve it through the normal Thought ->
Decision -> Cognition flow.
